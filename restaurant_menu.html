Feijoada


Feijoada is a traditional Brazilian stew made with black beans and different types of pork and beef. Complete Feijoada comes with more sides like rice and farofa, while Simply Feijoada is just the beans and meat. $15


Parmegiana

Parmegiana is a popular Brazilian dish made with breaded and fried meat, topped with tomato sauce and melted cheese. Meat Parmegiana is made with beef, while Chicken Parmegiana is made with chicken. $18


Virado a Paulista

Virado a Paulista is a traditional Brazilian dish made with rice, beans, pork, and a fried egg. It is typically served with bacon and sausage as additional toppings. $19
